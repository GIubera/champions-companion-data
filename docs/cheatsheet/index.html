The Pokémon Champions cheatsheet that does the thinking for you

You don't need a damage calculator and a spreadsheet to climb. Champions Companion tells you, in plain English, what beats what — built for new and casual players, not just tournament veterans.

How it works

  1. Scan the enemy's team preview. Screenshot their six at the 90-second preview — the app reads it on your device, no account, no upload.
  2. Get a plain-language game plan. Which four to bring, what can one-shot you, who you beat, and the safest lead — a clear green / yellow / red verdict per matchup.
  3. Glance at the overlay mid-battle. A floating window keeps your picks, threats and speed order one tap away.
