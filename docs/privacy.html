Privacy Policy — Champions Companion

Last updated: 13 June 2026

Champions Companion ("the app") is an unofficial, fan-made companion tool that helps players build teams and read matchup cheatsheets for the competitive battling game it supports. This policy explains how the app handles data.

Information we collect

The app does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email address, or any other personally identifying information. We do not run a server that stores personal data about you.

Data stored on your device

The teams you create and your in-app preferences are saved locally on your device only, using the device's standard local storage. This information never leaves your device and is deleted when you clear the app's data or uninstall the app.

Box screenshot scanning

If you use the box-scanning feature, the image you choose is processed entirely on your device to recognize species. Images are never uploaded to us or to any third party.

Game data updates

The app periodically downloads updated game data (the meta database) from a public, static, read-only endpoint. No personal information is sent with these requests beyond what any ordinary web request includes (such as your IP address, which is handled by the hosting provider and not stored by us).

Advertising (free version)

The free version of the app may display advertisements served by Google AdMob. When ads are enabled, Google and its partners may collect and use device identifiers and usage information to deliver and measure advertising, as described in Google's own policies. See the Google Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services. A one-time paid option removes ads.

Children

The app is intended for a general audience aged 13 and over and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. The "last updated" date above will reflect any changes.

Contact

For any question about this policy, you can contact us at the developer email shown on our Google Play Store listing.

This app is not affiliated with, endorsed, or sponsored by Nintendo, Game Freak, Creatures Inc., or The Pokémon Company. No official assets are included.
